How the Escrow System Works
1
Employer posts a job and sets the pay amount. Workers apply.
2
Employer hires a worker and pays the job amount into escrow via M-Pesa STK Push. Funds are held securely.
3
Worker completes the job. The employer confirms and releases the funds.
4
Worker receives payment via M-Pesa B2C transfer instantly. If there's a dispute, the employer can request a refund.
