Privacy Policy

This policy explains what personal information Kazi Mtaani collects, why we collect it, how it is used, and what rights you have over your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your information with care and respect.

Last updated: 27 May 2026  |  Effective: 27 May 2026

1 Who We Are

Kazi Mtaani is an online platform that connects informal workers with employers across Kenya. We are a Kenyan-owned platform operating at kazimtaani.co.ke.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at support@kazimtaani.co.ke.

2 What Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services:

  • Account information: Your full name and phone number when you register.
  • Profile information: Skills, years of experience, specialization, bio, and location (county and sub-county) that you voluntarily add to your profile.
  • Identity documents: Scanned copies of your national ID or other documents you upload to verify your identity. These are stored securely on Cloudinary.
  • Certificates and credentials: Professional certificates or qualifications you upload to your profile.
  • Job and payment activity: Jobs you post, apply for, or complete, and related payment status records.
  • Communications: Messages exchanged with other users through the platform.
  • Usage logs: Actions taken on the platform (logins, job posts, hires) recorded for security and audit purposes.

3 How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To match workers with suitable job opportunities based on skills and location.
  • To notify you via SMS and in-platform alerts about jobs that match your profile.
  • To enable secure payments through our manual M-PESA escrow system.
  • To allow employers and workers to communicate and review each other after job completion.
  • To verify your identity and display a verified badge on your profile.
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security threats on the platform.
  • To comply with our legal obligations under Kenyan law.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

4 SMS Notifications

By registering on Kazi Mtaani, you agree to receive SMS messages to the phone number you provide. These messages may include:

  • New job alerts matching your skills and location.
  • Notifications that you have been hired for a job.
  • One-time passwords (OTPs) for password reset.
  • Important account or payment updates.

SMS messages are sent via Africa's Talking, a licensed Kenyan telecommunications service provider. Standard mobile network message rates may apply.

You may request to opt out of job-alert SMS by contacting us at support@kazimtaani.co.ke.

5 Who We Share Your Information With

We share your information only when necessary:

  • Other users: Your public profile (name, skills, location, rating) is visible to employers searching for workers. Your phone number is only shared with an employer after you have been hired, to enable WhatsApp communication.
  • Africa's Talking: Your phone number is passed to this SMS provider solely to deliver notifications to you.
  • Cloudinary: Your uploaded documents and certificates are stored on Cloudinary's secure cloud servers.
  • MongoDB Atlas: Your account and activity data is stored on MongoDB Atlas, a cloud database with enterprise-grade security.
  • Legal authorities: We may disclose information if required to do so by Kenyan law, court order, or a lawful request from a government authority.

6 Data Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS/SSL encryption.
  • Passwords are stored using bcrypt hashing — we never store your password in plain text.
  • Authentication uses secure, httpOnly JWT cookies that cannot be accessed by browser scripts.
  • Identity documents are stored privately on Cloudinary and are only accessible to you and the Kazi Mtaani admin team.
  • All significant actions are recorded in a secure audit log.

7 Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account:

  • Your profile, job history, messages, and ratings are permanently deleted.
  • Identity documents are deleted from Cloudinary.
  • Audit log entries related to your account are retained for a maximum of 12 months for security compliance, then deleted.

8 Your Rights Under the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019

As a data subject under the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information through your profile settings.
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time using the "Delete Account" option in your dashboard.
  • Object to the processing of your data for specific purposes.
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account or contacting us.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner of Kenya if you believe your rights have been violated.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@kazimtaani.co.ke. We will respond within 14 days.

9 Children's Privacy

Kazi Mtaani is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a minor has registered on our platform, please contact us immediately at support@kazimtaani.co.ke.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will notify registered users via SMS and update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
